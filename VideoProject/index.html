Video Project

When I found out Sooah was in Creative Writing, we decided to pair up to create a hurdling tutorial. Since we're both on the track team, we would be able to spend a practice session getting footage of him learning how to do hurdles. We would also be able to get other real hurdlers to show off what proper form should look like.

The video goes over an overview of hurdles to stretches, basic lead-leg form, trail-leg form, and some demonstrations of what it should look like combined all together.

Made with hugs and kisses!
